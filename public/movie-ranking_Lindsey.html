The Best Movies According to Lindsey

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Wonderful life

This is a classic christmas film in 1945, it's about how wonderful life it can be, everything happens for a good purpose.

Life is beautiful

A great movie how a father give his little boy the hope and positive side of the world.

The pig at the crossing

A film by Khyentse Norbu, about samsara, and how a thought can be an attachment even after death.
